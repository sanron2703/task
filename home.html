Saree Kuchu & Tassels

Saree Kuchu also referred as Saree Tassels is a traditional form of handcrafting beautiful frills & embellishments to the silk saree pallu..

Saree tasselling, an hobby that we inherited from our moms was revived to suit the current fashion trend and labelled under the brand name Rooney in 2017.

Rooney’s saree kuchus are inspired by the royal motifs in the silk sarees and offers more than 175 designs to choose from.

All kuchus are meticulously handcrafted using premium silk yarn and uniquely stringed together to complement your beautiful sarees.

Rooney™ - An art startup for the women..by the women..!!

Saree Kuchu Workshop

Rooney has been instrumental in promoting this traditional craft through the saree kuchu learning workshops.

More than 800 women from far away districts of Karnataka & other states have learnt this beautiful craft of saree kuchu making through the workshops conducted at Rooney, Mysuru.
